## Export Controls / Tech

**US BIS closes "50% loophole" and revises semi licensing for China**
- **Change**: BIS issued a final rule (effective Feb 18) automatically applying Entity List/MEU restrictions to any company 50% owned by a listed entity, and revised licensing to "case-by-case" for Nvidia H200/AMD MI325X exports to China (previously presumptive denial).
- **Affected**: Global supply chains, Nvidia/AMD (revenue recognition), Chinese AI compute access.
- **Market channel**: Equities (Semis/Tech), SupplyChains (compliance overhead).
