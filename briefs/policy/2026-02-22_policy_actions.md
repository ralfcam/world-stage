# Policy Actions: February 22, 2026

## US
- **Trump raises global tariff to 15% (Section 122) following Supreme Court defeat**
  - **Change**: President Trump announced an increase of the new global tariff to 15% (maximum allowed under Section 122), up from the 10% announced Friday, replacing the IEEPA-based tariffs struck down by the Supreme Court.
  - **Affected**: Global imports to the US (exemptions for USMCA/FTA partners unclear in new proclamation).
  - **Market channel**: FX (USD structural bid), Rates (inflation breakevens), Equities (margin compression).
  - **Link**: [White House Proclamation 10892](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2026/02/imposing-a-temporary-import-surcharge-to-address-fundamental-international-payments-problems/)
  - **Tags**: [Regulation][Geoeconomics][Macro]
  - **Continuity**: New (Legal basis shift to Trade Act of 1974)
