## Trade / Tariffs

**US invokes Section 122 (Trade Act) for 15% global import surcharge**
- **Change**: President Trump signed Executive Order invoking Section 122 "Balance of Payments" authority to impose a temporary global import surcharge (initially 10%, raised to 15% on Feb 21) effective Feb 24, replacing IEEPA tariffs.
- **Affected**: Global imports to US; exemptions for critical minerals, energy, pharmaceuticals, and USMCA/Section 232 goods.
- **Market channel**: Rates (inflation impulse), FX (USD strength), Equities (margin compression).
- **Link**: [White House Fact Sheet](https://www.whitehouse.gov/presidential-actions/2026/02/imposing-a-temporary-import-surcharge-to-address-fundamental-international-payments-problems/)
- **Tags**: [Regulation][Trade][Macro]
- **Continuity**: New (ref: 2026-02-20)

**SCOTUS strikes down IEEPA-based global tariffs (6-3)**
- **Change**: Supreme Court ruled that IEEPA does not grant presidential authority to impose broad global tariffs, invalidating previous executive orders (Learning Resources, Inc. v. Trump).
- **Affected**: Presidential emergency powers authority (IEEPA); immediate pivot to Section 122.
- **Market channel**: Equities (rule of law/volatility), Rates (uncertainty premium).
- **Link**: [Supreme Court Opinion](https://www.supremecourt.gov/)
- **Tags**: [Regulation][Legal][Trade]
- **Continuity**: Reversal (ref: 2026-02-20)
