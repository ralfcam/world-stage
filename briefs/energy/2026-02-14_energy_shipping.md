# Energy + Shipping Risk Premium
**Date:** 2026-02-14
**Status:** Volatility spike; "Diplomacy" trade clashes with on-water friction

---

## Executive Summary

Energy markets are caught in a violent repricing tug-of-war. Brent crude crashed >5% to <$63 before rebounding to ~$67 as "serious talks" between the US and Iran (mediated by Oman) collide with on-the-water reality (IRGC interception attempts earlier in Feb). The "Peace Trade" is currently dominant but fragile. Shipping risks in the Red Sea remain structural (100+ days without Houthi attacks, yet traffic remains -60%), while Taiwan Strait tensions simmer without acute escalation. EU Gas remains the stable anchor (~€32/MWh) despite colder weather forecasts.
